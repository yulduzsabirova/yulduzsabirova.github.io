Welcome! I am a second-year PhD student at the University *of* Pittsburgh's School *of* Public and International Affairs. My research focuses on political economy and resource governance in Central Asia, particularly cooperation over shared water and other natural resources.


I hold a Master of Public Affairs from the O'Neill School *of* Public and Environmental Affairs at Indiana University, a Master's in Industrial Economics from Changchun University *of* Science and Technology, and a Specialist degree in Economics and Management from the Stary Oskol branch of MISIS.
